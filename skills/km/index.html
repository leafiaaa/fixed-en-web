xNinja.Knowledge Management

Knowledge Management (KM) creates, collects, and manages organizational knowledge assets, enabling employees to quickly access the information they need, fostering collaboration and learning, and enhancing overall productivity and innovation.

FEATURES

Diverse Knowledge Type Creation

  • Add, Manage & Publish: Provide functions for adding, managing, and publishing diverse knowledge content (such as articles, albums, videos, files, and websites) to meet the company's diverse needs in knowledge presentation.

Strict Knowledge Access Control

  • Dual Protection: Set permissions and access levels based on knowledge types to provide dual protection for the company's critical knowledge assets.
  • Change Safeguards: Ensures that important organizational knowledge cannot be arbitrarily modified or deleted.

Full-Text Search Keyword

  • Built-In Search Engine: Provides full-text search functionality for knowledge points.
  • Usage Analytics: Offers keyword clouds and graphical statistical reports, enabling the organization to accurately track usage needs, frequency, and departments of the knowledge.

Add and Modification Strict Review

  • Integrated Audit Mechanism: The addition and modification management of important knowledge can be integrated with the organization's internal audit mechanism for process design and responsibility management.
  • Review Workflow: This includes tasks such as approval, countersignature, assignment, and notification.
